# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- Business owners of Indonesian SMBs (workshops, retail, services) who need to automate CS.
- Customer service (CS) administrators who handle handoff tickets and answer complex chats manually.
- Sales and social media staff who monitor customer feedback and comments.

## Product Purpose
Balesin AI provides a professional omnichannel AI customer service platform. It centralizes messages from WhatsApp, Instagram, and web chat into a unified inbox, using a controlled AI workflow for common questions and routing high-risk or low-confidence questions to human CS agents.

## Brand Personality
- Professional and Reliable: Instills trust in automation and security.
- Modern and Clean: Visual systems are simple, efficient, and direct.
- Friendly and Accessible: Localized, easy to understand, and fast to operate for Indonesian SMB staff.

## Anti-references
- SaaS Cliché template: Dark navy background with bright cyan/purple radial glow gradients.
- Generic AI default: Pairing geometric display fonts (Space Grotesk) with monospace body fonts (IBM Plex Mono).
- Glassmorphism defaults: Blurs and translucent glass cards used for decorative layouts.
- Hero metrics: Huge stats numbers with gradient accents and tiny subtext labels.
- Identical card grids: Repeating the exact same cards with icons and body text.
- Tiny uppercase tracked eyebrows: "ABOUT", "PLATFORM", "MODULES" kickers above every header.
- Numbered sections (01, 02, 03) on non-sequential content.

## Design Principles
1. Restrained and Purposeful: Design elements serve the user's workflow. Colors represent state and hierarchy, not decoration.
2. Premium Contrast and Readability: Dark-mode neutral tones are carefully chosen using clean gray scales with sufficient contrast (>= 4.5:1).
3. Component Familiarity: Rely on standard, predictable UI affordances that users trust (inspired by Stripe, Linear, and Notion).
4. Localized Simplicity: Indonesian terminology is clean, natural, and clear.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Target: WCAG 2.1 AA compliance.
- Text contrast must exceed 4.5:1.
- Clear outline or ring indicator on keyboard focus states.
- Animations must be minimal and respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
